Welcome to the ITCA scientists section

The Indian Technology Congress Association (ITCA) is a non-profit professional organization established in 2013 and based in Bengaluru, India. It focuses on promoting innovation and collaboration in the fields of space technology, engineering, and education. ITCA plays a key role in connecting industry, academia, and government through initiatives like the Indian Technology Congress. One of its major projects is the 75 Student Satellites Mission, launched in collaboration with ISRO and various educational institutions to celebrate Indiaâ€™s 75th year of independence. ITCA actively supports and guides students in building and launching CubeSats (miniature satellites), fostering hands-on experience in space research and development.
