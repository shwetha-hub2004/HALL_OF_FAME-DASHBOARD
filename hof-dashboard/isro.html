Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)

ISRO is the space agency of the Government of India, responsible for the nation's space program. Since its formation in 1969, ISRO has made groundbreaking achievements in satellite launches, interplanetary missions, and space applications.

1969

ISRO was founded by Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, ushering in a new era of Indian space research.

1975

Launched India's first satellite – Aryabhata.

2013

Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan) successfully launched.

2023

Chandrayaan-3 lands successfully near the Moon’s south pole.
